Avoid the Snake

In this game, there are two players: the protagonist and the snake.
The protagonist's goal is to reach a score of 25 before being killed by the snake, while the
snake's wishes to kill the protagonist before they reach that objective.


In order to collect points, the protagonist must eat apples. However, the snake can also eat apples.
Depending on which type of apple is eaten and by which player, an additional
effect is achieved each time one is eaten.


Red apples add one to the score when eaten by the protagonist, and add one unit to the snake if eaten by the snake.
Life apples increment the score by one and give the player an extra life if consumed by the player, while growing the snake if consumed by the snake.
Lime apples add 5 to the score when eaten by the protagonist, and subtracts one from the score while still growing the snake when eaten by the snake.
Golden apples add 3 to the score and shrink the snake back down to it's original size when eaten by the protagonist,
and grow the snake by two units when eaten by the snake.
Death apples are extremely rare black apples that ensure victory for the consumer, setting the score to 100 if eaten by the protagonist
and setting the score to 0 and teleporting the protagonist into the snake's clutches if eaten by the snake.


The protagonist uses the arrow pad to move, while the snake uses WASD to move and cannot change direction directly 180 degrees from it's current trajectory.
